      Finding an ideal charming small hotel in Noida does not have to be difficult. Welcome to CSS Abode, a nice option for travellers like you. ISKCON Noida (2.9 km), located nearby, makes CSS Abode a great place to stay for those interested in visiting this popular Noida landmark. Free wifi is offered to guests, and rooms at CSS Abode offer air conditioning, a minibar, and a desk. During your stay, take advantage of some of the amenities offered, including room service and executive lounge access. For travellers arriving by car, free parking is available. While in Noida be sure to experience nearby Thai restaurants such as Berco's, R.E.D., or Ching Shihh. Noida is also home to Escapade Adventures and Holidays Pvt. Ltd - Day Tour, a popular architectural building that is not too far from CSS Abode. CSS Abode looks forward to welcoming you on your visit to Noida.
